Greedy Algorithm Introduction

Greedy Algorithm is a problem-solving approach
where we make the best choice at each step
to get the overall optimal solution.

It focuses on making a local optimal choice
with the hope that it leads to a global optimum.

Basic Idea:

- At each step, pick the best available option
- Do not reconsider previous choices
- Build solution step by step

How it works:

- Break problem into steps
- Choose the best option at each step
- Continue until solution is complete

Example:

Coin Change Problem

Coins: 1, 2, 5, 10
Amount: 18

Greedy Approach:
Pick largest coin first

10 → remaining 8  
5 → remaining 3  
2 → remaining 1  
1 → done  

Result: 4 coins
